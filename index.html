How it works

Connected in under a minute

1

Install both apps

The KD CONNECT app on your Android phone, and the desktop app on your Windows laptop.

2

Set up the phone once

A guided 2-step setup enables the Accessibility service (cursor & taps) and the KD CONNECT keyboard (typing).

3

Pair your way

Scan the QR code, pick your phone from the list, or plug in a USB cable — then tap Allow on the phone.

4

Press F9

Your mouse and keyboard now drive the phone. Press F9 again to take them back. That's it.

Two ways to connect

Wi-Fi for convenience. USB for speed.

Use one at a time and switch whenever you like — they never conflict.

EASIEST

Wi-Fi

  • No cable — pair by QR, discovery or address
  • Low-latency mode + smart packet pacing built in
  • Great on a quiet network
  • Speed depends on your Wi-Fi — busy networks can lag
FASTEST

USB cable

  • Feels like a wired mouse (~1–2 ms)
  • Immune to Wi-Fi congestion — never lags
  • Works even with Wi-Fi turned off
  • One-time setup: enable USB debugging (guided)

FAQ

Questions, answered

Is this screen mirroring?

No. Your phone's screen stays on the phone. KD CONNECT sends only your mouse and keyboard input, so you use the phone directly — with a cursor.

Can someone else control my phone?

No. Every new laptop must be approved on the phone with an Allow prompt before it can do anything. You can disconnect or turn the service off at any time, and a green dot is always visible while control is active.

Does it collect my data?

Nothing is collected. There are no accounts, no analytics and no servers — input travels directly between your laptop and phone on your own network or cable. See the privacy policy in the app.

The mouse feels laggy on Wi-Fi. What can I do?

Busy Wi-Fi networks add delay. KD CONNECT already keeps your phone's Wi-Fi in low-latency mode and paces its traffic, but for guaranteed smoothness use USB mode — it's immune to network congestion — or connect the laptop to your phone's hotspot.

Can I type passwords?

Yes — typing uses real key events, which work in password fields too. The KD CONNECT keyboard must be active while you type; your normal keyboard is restored automatically afterwards.

How many devices can I connect?

One phone and one laptop at a time — by design, so it's always clear who is in control. Switching to another device or transport disconnects the previous one cleanly.

Why does Windows warn me when I run the app?

The app isn't code-signed with a paid certificate yet, so SmartScreen shows a notice for any new download. Choose “More info → Run anyway”. The app is free, runs entirely on your own devices, and sends nothing to any server.
